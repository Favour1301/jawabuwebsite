Montessori Carriculum

Jawabu School is a beacon of excellence in early childhood education, offering a nurturing Montessori experience for young learners. Our approach goes beyond traditional teaching, inspiring curiosity, independence, and a lifelong love of learning from the very start.

At Jawabu School, our commitment to early childhood excellence is reflected in the Montessori curriculum, designed to help children grow holistically. Here’s why our Montessori program stands out:

Child-Centered Exploration: Every child learns at their own pace. Our Montessori classrooms provide a carefully prepared environment where children can explore, experiment, and discover, fostering independence and self-confidence.

Hands-On Learning: Learning through play and practical activities is at the heart of Montessori education. Children engage with sensory materials and real-life experiences that build fine motor skills, problem-solving abilities, and early cognitive development.

Social and Emotional Growth: Our Montessori approach nurtures not just the mind but also the heart. Through collaborative activities and guided interactions, children develop empathy, communication skills, and positive social behaviors.

Foundational Academic Skills: While academics are introduced gently, they are integrated into meaningful, hands-on experiences. Children build literacy, numeracy, and creative thinking in a natural, enjoyable way.

Individualized Learning: Every child is unique. Our teachers observe and guide each learner, tailoring activities to their interests and developmental stage, ensuring every child feels supported and challenged appropriately.

Seamless Early Education Transition: By cultivating independence, focus, and self-motivation, our Montessori graduates are well-prepared for primary school, equipped with the confidence and skills to thrive academically and socially.

At Jawabu School, the Montessori curriculum is more than a teaching method—it’s a joyful journey of discovery, growth, and empowerment for every child.

Competency Based Education CBE

Jawabu School is a beacon of excellence in early childhood education, offering a nurturing learning experience aligned with the Competency-Based Education (CBE) model. Our approach goes beyond traditional teaching, inspiring curiosity, creativity, and a lifelong love of learning from the very start.

Learner-Centered Approach: Every child is unique and learns at their own pace. Our classrooms provide a supportive environment where learners actively participate in their own learning journey, building confidence, independence, and responsibility.

Competency Development: We focus on nurturing core competencies such as communication, collaboration, critical thinking, creativity, and digital literacy. Learners are guided to apply knowledge in real-life situations, making learning meaningful and practical.

Hands-On and Experiential Learning: Learning is activity-based and engaging. Through play, projects, and practical experiences, learners develop problem-solving skills, innovation, and deeper understanding of concepts.

Values and Social Development: Our CBE approach emphasizes character building. Learners develop values such as respect, responsibility, integrity, and teamwork, preparing them to become responsible members of society.

Continuous Assessment: Assessment is ongoing and focuses on individual progress. Teachers observe and guide each learner, ensuring they master competencies before moving forward.

Inclusive and Individualized Learning: Every learner is supported according to their abilities and interests. Our teachers provide personalized guidance to ensure each child achieves their full potential.

Preparation for Future Learning: By developing essential competencies and life skills, our learners are well-prepared for the next levels of education and equipped to thrive in a dynamic world.

At Jawabu School, the CBE curriculum is more than a teaching method—it is a journey of discovery, growth, and empowerment.
